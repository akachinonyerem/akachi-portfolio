My design Process

The first thing I do is to understand the problem. Then I research the problem. If the solution involves an audience, I try to understand the audience and their perceived pain points too.

I analyze findings and use these findings to offer solution. I rethink and rework the solution to improve it.

Next step is reviewing and testing; using feedback and improve the solution before publishing it for everyone to use... because design isn't just color and pixels, it's about solving a problem and making life just a little bit better.
